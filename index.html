About Me

Hello! I am Guangzhe Wang, a third-year PhD candidate in Accounting at the School of Accounting and Finance, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research focuses on information disclosure and the application of AI, particularly large language models (LLMs), in finance and accounting research. My ongoing work explores how AI shapes the information environment of capital markets and influences individual behavior.

Prior to my PhD, I received a Master of Science in Data Science and Analytics. I am always open to collaboration and intellectual exchange, feel free to get in touch.
