About Me

Hello! I am Guangzhe Wang, a third-year Ph.D. candidate in Accounting at the School of Accounting and Finance, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research focuses on information disclosure and applies the artificial intelligence, particularly large language models, to questions in finance and accounting. My ongoing work explores how AI shapes the information environment of capital markets and influences individual behavior.

Prior to my Ph.D., I received a Master of Science in Data Science and Analytics. I am always open to collaboration and intellectual exchange, feel free to get in touch.
